Mesozoic Era Timeline

Journey through 186 million years of prehistoric history and discover how dinosaurs evolved and dominated Earth.

252 - 201 Million Years Ago

Triassic Period

The dawn of the dinosaur age. Early dinosaurs emerged alongside the first mammals. The supercontinent Pangaea began to break apart, creating new environments and evolutionary opportunities.

First Dinosaurs Coelophysis Plateosaurus
201 - 145 Million Years Ago

Jurassic Period

The golden age of dinosaurs. Massive sauropods roamed lush forests while fierce predators like Allosaurus hunted. The first birds took flight, evolving from small theropod dinosaurs.

Brachiosaurus Allosaurus First Birds
145 - 66 Million Years Ago

Cretaceous Period

The final chapter of the dinosaur era. T-Rex and Triceratops battled while flowering plants transformed landscapes. This period ended with the mass extinction that wiped out non-avian dinosaurs.

T-Rex Triceratops Mass Extinction

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for your prehistoric adventure

What are your park hours?

We're open daily from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM during peak season (June-August) and 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM during off-season. Extended hours on weekends and holidays.

Is the park wheelchair accessible?

Yes! All major attractions and pathways are fully accessible. We provide complimentary wheelchair rentals and have accessible restrooms throughout the park.

What's your weather policy?

Most attractions operate rain or shine. In severe weather, some outdoor rides may temporarily close for safety. We offer full refunds for closures exceeding 4 hours.

Can I bring food and drinks?

Outside food is welcome! We have designated picnic areas and water fountains throughout the park. Glass containers and alcohol are not permitted for safety reasons.

Are there height restrictions?

Some thrill rides have minimum height requirements (36-48 inches) for safety. We have plenty of family-friendly attractions suitable for all ages and heights.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 15+ receive 20% off admission. School groups and birthday parties get special rates. Contact our group sales team for custom packages.
